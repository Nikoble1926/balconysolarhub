Contact

Quick answer: Email us at hello@balconysolarhub.co.uk for corrections, feedback, story tips, or partnership enquiries. We respond within a few working days. Independent site; we do not accept paid product placement.

What we’d love to hear about

  • Stale facts — if a regulation, date, or product detail on the site is out of date, please tell us with a primary source if possible.
  • BSI / Amendment 4 updates — we’re tracking the product-standard rollout closely; tips on publication or transitional-period changes are very welcome.
  • DNO portal changes — if a DNO has moved their G98 page, let us know.
  • UK kit availability — if a kit we list as “watch” is now reliably in stock, or vice versa.
  • Real install stories — first-hand UK installs (cost, electrician, DNO experience) help future readers a lot.
